Effective Date: July 13, 2026

At Francisco Painting and General Construction, we respect the privacy of our website visitors and clients. This Privacy Policy outlines the types of information we collect, how we use and protect that information, and your legal rights under California privacy regulations.

1. Information Collection

We collect personal information that you voluntarily provide when submitting estimate request forms, sending emails, or calling our local office. This information may include:

  • Full Name
  • Email Address
  • Phone Number
  • Physical Project Address
  • Project Details and Layout Budgets

2. Cookies & Tracking Technologies

We use standard client-side browser cookies to improve your user experience and navigate between our multi-page portals smoothly. Cookies allow us to analyze traffic flows, remember page states, and provide localized maps or search tools.

3. Analytics Tools

We utilize standard website analytic software (such as Google Analytics) to compile aggregate data about user interactions, page-load performance, first contentful paint (FCP), and traffic referrals. This information does not contain individually identifiable credentials and is purely used to refine site speed and usability.

4. Third Party Services

Our website displays embedded maps from Google Maps and connects to our corporate listings on the Google Knowledge Graph. These third-party providers may collect cookies or device identifiers in accordance with their respective privacy policies.

5. California User Rights

Under California Consumer Privacy Acts, local state residents hold specific privacy rights, including:

  • The right to know what personal data is collected and processed.
  • The right to request deletion of voluntarily submitted information.
  • The right to opt-out of data sharing (We do NOT sell or lease user information).

6. Data Protection

We implement appropriate physical, technical, and digital security measures to guard your voluntarily provided information against unauthorized disclosure, loss, or alteration.
