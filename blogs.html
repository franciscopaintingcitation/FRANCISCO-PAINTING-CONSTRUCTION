Construction Tips

Structural Framework, Framing & Engineering: Crucial Construction Tips

Behind every gorgeous plaster wall and premium coat of paint lies the skeleton of your home—the structural framing. If the rough **carpentry framing**, **load-bearing headers**, or floor joists are miscalculated, walls will warp, windows will stick, and ceilings will sag.

Always insist on kiln-dried Douglas Fir timber studs, and double check that all structural modifications are reviewed by certified **structural engineers**. You can inspect completed **home additions** and **structural framing** photos on the franciscoconstruction.com official website, or check our reviews at our Google GMB map page.

Additionally, remember to plan your mechanical, plumbing, and electrical rough-ins before locking down your **drywall panels**, as post-construction utility routing can be expensive.

Premium Painting

The Master Painter's Guide to Exterior Color Longevity in Sunny California

Southern California weather offers breathtaking sunny days, but it also exposes your home's **exterior painting** to brutal ultraviolet radiation, thermal expansion, and coastal moisture. Cheap paints degrade rapidly, leading to chalking, fading, and bubbling within just a few seasons.

To preserve your home's luxury curb appeal, always invest in premium 100% acrylic latex paint formulas (such as Sherwin-Williams Emerald or Benjamin Moore Aura) and demand a meticulous three-step preparation routine: power washing, structural caulking of wood siding seams, and high-performance primer bonding.
