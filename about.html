Since 2004

22+ Years of Structural Innovation in North Hills

Francisco Painting and General Construction was founded on the belief that custom home building is an art form. Over the last two decades, our dedicated team of local carpenters, drywall experts, painters, and framing engineers has served property owners throughout Los Angeles and Ventura County with unwavering distinction.

Under owner-operator oversight, we have grown from a boutique residential painter into a highly respected general contracting firm, completing premium kitchen additions, zero-threshold spa bathrooms, multi-room renovations, external cedar siding, and intricate decking architectures.

We believe in absolute clarity: we work with honest timelines, premium-tier materials, full structural engineering permits, and client coordination software so you know your project is in master hands.

Methodology

The Four Pillars of Construction Success

Our master-planned workflow guarantees your home additions and painting projects are delivered on time, within budget, and with zero architectural defects.

01

Architectural Consult

We analyze your home's layout, evaluate mechanical integrations (HVAC, plumbing, electrical load), map structural goals, and finalize highly detailed blueprints and flat-rate pricing estimates.

02

Permits & Site Prep

Our managers obtain municipal building clearances, coordinate civil engineers, verify utility line easements, and establish a clean, safe, and secure containment workspace.

03

Framing & Structural

Our master carpenters execute structural framing, install code-compliant header beams, fit high-performance windows, secure cedar/cement siding, and integrate skylight frames.

Commitment To Uncompromising Durability

Every piece of structural Douglas Fir timber, every bucket of premium Sherwin-Williams Emerald paint, and every fixture we mount is selected for local resilience, eco-friendliness, and pristine aesthetic alignment. We stand by our work with an extended structural warranty.
